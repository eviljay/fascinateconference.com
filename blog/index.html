15 June is Final Extension for Proposals!

Fantastic response to proposals for Conference Papers and Showcase Performance/Installation Proposals.. thank you to all around the World for interest in Fascinate 2014 http://www.fascinateconference.com/

Inevitably we have had many requests from latecomers for a final extension and we have therefore extended an absolute final deadline for Papers and Showcase to Sunday 15th June. This will be the final date and anything received after will not be considered for 2014!
