FASCINATE 2014 : 27-31 August : Falmouth – Cornwall – England

In 2014 Fascinate runs in parallel with the Falmouth Tall Ships Regatta for which there will be ample opportunities for delegates at Fascinate showcase, workshops and conference to interact with!
FASCINATE is an interdisciplinary conference investigating the current and future applications of ubiquitous computing technologies in visual and performance arts, games, architecture, craft, design and interactive media.
FASCINATE explores technology, design and experience related to ubiquitous computing. Areas of interest include: ambient intelligence; experience design; cognitive environments; augmented performance; pervasive media and the internet of things.
FASCINATE offers participants the opportunity to present and discuss their work, inspire and be inspired by the work of others across a range of fields of practice; build on the experience of keynote speakers and establish new and eclectic collaborations.

  • The application of craft in digital games
  • Digital technologies in music, theatre and dance performance

  • The use of digital applications in location based practices
  • Current developments in networked and immersive performances

Thematic Areas

  • Ambient Computing and Cognitive Environments
  • Tangible, haptic and embodied Interfaces
  • Ubiquitous computing and architecture
  • User fabrication of 3d and electronic devices
  • Innovative interfaces in contemporary arts and crafts
  • Projection Mapping

  • Augmented musical instruments
  • Interfaces for dance and theatre
  • Networked performance and telepresence
  • Motion and gesture analysis
  • Augmented Reality
  • Digital games development & applications
